omniStock – Zambian Business Accountant
OmniStock is a comprehensive inventory and accounting management platform designed for Zambian businesses. It enables SMEs, retailers, and accountants to track stock, scan barcodes, and leverage AI-powered insights to improve efficiency, reduce losses, and make informed financial decisions.

Key Features
- Inventory Management – Real-time tracking of stock levels, movements, and valuations.
- Barcode Scanning – Fast product identification with reduced manual errors.
- AI Insights – Forecast demand, detect anomalies, and optimize operations.
- Accounting Integration – Manage expenses, revenue, and financial reports seamlessly.
- Localized for Zambia – Tailored to Zambian business needs, including compliance and contextual insights.
Business Benefits
- Reduce stock losses through accurate monitoring.
- Improve cash flow visibility with integrated accounting.
- Save time and reduce errors with automation.
- Gain competitive advantage through AI-powered forecasts.
- Empower managers and accountants with clear dashboards and reports.
